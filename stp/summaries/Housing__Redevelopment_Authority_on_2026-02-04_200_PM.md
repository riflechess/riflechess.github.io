#### Meeting Info

* **Attendance:** Five commissioners present (Kim, Noecker, Yang, Vice Chair Jost, and one absent Commissioner), with two excused absences (Johnson and Bowie)

#### Summary
The meeting began with the approval of the January 2026 HRA meeting minutes (MIN 26-7). The item was approved unanimously.

Next, a public hearing was held on Resolution RESPH 26-33, which authorized the issuance and sale of conduit healthcare revenue bonds for two projects: Episcopal Homes Obligated Group's Willows Project at 80 McCubbin Street in Ward 1. Director Green and Colin Ibley, controller of Episcopal Homes, were present to answer questions.
