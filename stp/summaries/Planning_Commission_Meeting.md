#### Meeting Info
**Type:** Planning Commission Meeting
**Purpose:** Review and discussion of city development projects, zoning proposals, and planning initiatives
**Date/Time:** Tuesday, March 21st, 2023 at 9:00 AM
**Location:** City Hall, Room 300

#### Summary
The meeting began with a review of the agenda, which included several development proposals, zoning variances, and an update on the city's comprehensive plan. Key discussions include:

* **West Side Development Proposal**: A mixed-use development project was presented, proposing a new high-rise building with retail and residential space. The commission discussed concerns about density, parking, and community engagement.
* **Zoning Variance Requests**: Two zoning variance requests were considered for single-family homes in the Summit-University neighborhood. One request was approved unanimously, while the other was tabled due to concerns about the impact on adjacent properties.

A contentious discussion arose during the review of a proposed development project near the Mississippi River. Commissioner Johnson expressed concerns about the potential strain on local infrastructure and the lack of community input in the planning process. Commissioner Rodriguez countered that the proposal met all necessary zoning requirements and would bring much-needed affordable housing to the area.

The commission voted 6-2 in favor of moving forward with the development project, with Commissioners Johnson and Thompson dissenting.
