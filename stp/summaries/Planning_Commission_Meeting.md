#### Meeting Info
* **Meeting Type:** Planning Commission Meeting
* **Purpose:** To discuss and make decisions on land use, zoning, and development projects in the city
* **Date/Time:** Tuesday, March 14, 2023, 2:00 PM
* **Location:** City Hall, Saint Paul

#### Summary
The Planning Commission meeting covered several key topics. 

* The commission discussed a proposal to rezone a 5-acre plot of land in the East Side area for a new mixed-use development. After some discussion, they voted to approve the rezoning with a 6-1 vote.
	+ Commissioner Rodriguez expressed concerns about potential traffic congestion and parking issues near the site.
	+ Commissioner Thompson countered that the development would bring much-needed affordable housing and retail space to the area.
* The commission also discussed a proposal to create a new bike lane on Summit Avenue. They voted to approve the plan, but with some modifications to address concerns from local business owners about potential parking losses.
* A resident spoke out about noise pollution from a nearby industrial site, requesting that the city take action to mitigate the issue.

Note: Commissioner Rodriguez's concerns were the only dissenting vote in both cases where votes occurred.
