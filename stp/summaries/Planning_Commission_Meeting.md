#### Meeting Info

* **Meeting Type:** Planning Commission Meeting
* **Purpose:** Review and discuss proposed developments, zoning changes, and other land-use matters in Saint Paul.
* **Date/Time:** Tuesday, March 21st, 2023, 6:00 PM - 9:30 PM (CST)
* **Location:** City Hall, Council Chambers, 300 D Street NE, Saint Paul, MN 55101

#### Summary

The Planning Commission Meeting on March 21st focused on several key agenda items:

* The commission reviewed and approved the preliminary plat for the proposed redevelopment of a vacant lot in the Frogtown neighborhood. This project includes plans for mixed-income housing, retail spaces, and community facilities.
* A zoning text amendment was introduced to establish new guidelines for short-term rental properties (e.g., Airbnb) within Saint Paul's residential areas. The proposal aims to balance property rights with concerns about noise, parking, and community character.

A contentious discussion arose regarding the proposed development of a former industrial site along the Mississippi River. Some commission members expressed concerns about potential environmental impacts and the need for more public input in the planning process. However, proponents argued that the project would revitalize an underutilized area, create jobs, and enhance the city's riverfront.

No votes were taken during this meeting; further discussion and action will be considered at a future Planning Commission Meeting.
