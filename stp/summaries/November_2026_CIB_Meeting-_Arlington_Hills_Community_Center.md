#### Meeting Info
* **Meeting Type:** Committee of the Interior Board (CIB) meeting
* **Purpose:** Regular meeting to discuss various city projects, initiatives, and concerns
* **Date/Time:** November 2026
* **Location:** Arlington Hills Community Center
* **Attendees:** Members of the CIB, City officials, and the public

#### Summary

The Committee of the Interior Board (CIB) met at the Arlington Hills Community Center on November 2026. The meeting covered several topics, including:

* **Arlington Hills Park Improvements**: The city is planning to renovate the park's playground equipment and add new lighting. A budget of $150,000 was allocated for this project.
* **Business License Fees**: The committee discussed increasing business license fees to generate more revenue for the city. However, some members expressed concerns about the potential impact on small businesses.
* **Safety Concerns**: Residents expressed concerns about crime in the area, particularly around the corner of Summit and Snelling. The police department assured the public that they are taking steps to increase patrols in the area.
* **Budget Allocation**: The committee reviewed the city's budget for 2027 and made some adjustments, including allocating $200,000 for a new community program.

There was no unanimous vote on the business license fee increase, with three members voting against it. The main concern was that it would place an undue burden on small businesses in the area. However, the majority of the committee agreed that the increased revenue would be beneficial to the city's finances.

Overall, the meeting focused on improving public spaces and addressing community concerns.
