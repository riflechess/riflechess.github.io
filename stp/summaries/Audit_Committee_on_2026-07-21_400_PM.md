#### Meeting Info
**Type:** Audit Committee Meeting
**Purpose:** Review of community engagement progress and planning for upcoming outreach to civic organizations.
**Date/Time:** July 21, 2026, at 4:00 PM

#### Summary
The committee reviewed recent efforts regarding community outreach and risk assessment. A representative reported successful cooperation from the district councils, noting that they were able to connect with nearly all of them—either in person or via written correspondence—to gather input for future consideration.

Looking forward, a plan was established to begin meeting with various civic organizations starting in the fall. The intended outreach list includes several key groups:
*   League of Women Voters, St. Paul and Minnesota Foundation
*   Citizen League and Chamber of Commerce
*   Neighborhood House and Isaiah
*   Asian Economic Development Association
*   Brown Community Center, The Alliance, Downtown Alliance, Open Arms, and Star House.

**Points of Note:**
During the discussion regarding these civic organizations, a request was made to ensure the committee reaches out to groups that may be more critical of city initiatives, rather than only those traditionally "friendly" to the city. Specific suggestions were made to include **Insight St. Paul**, **St. Paul Strong**, and local neighborhood-specific groups like the **Railroad Island Neighborhood Group**. The committee agreed to expand and prioritize this list to ensure a broader range of voices are heard in future discussions.
