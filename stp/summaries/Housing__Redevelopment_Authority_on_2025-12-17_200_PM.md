#### Meeting Info
**Meeting Type:** Housing and Redevelopment Authority (HRA) meeting
**Purpose:** Recognize long-time employees' service, discuss various projects and initiatives, and review year-end report
**Date/Time:** December 17, 2025, 2:00 PM
**Relevant Context:** The meeting was attended by several HRA commissioners and staff members, including Director McMahon

#### Summary

The meeting began with the recognition of three employees who are retiring or have recently retired. Paul Dubriel has served for 30 years, Ross Courier has served for 11 years, and Marie Franchette has served for 32 years. Resolutions were passed to honor their service.

Marie Franchette's resolution was particularly notable, as it highlighted her extensive career in public service, including leading the redevelopment of the West Side Flats and the Penfield project. Director McMahon presented a detailed account of Marie's achievements, which included successfully negotiating with developers, managing large-scale projects, and advocating for climate resiliency and equity.

The meeting also featured a presentation by Director McMahon on the HRA's year-end report. The report highlighted various accomplishments, including:

* Successful audits
* Completion of several major development sites, such as United Village and Highland Bridge
* Launch of the Commercial Corridor Organization Assistance Program
* Progress on sustainability initiatives, including multifamily housing projects that comply with the Sustainable Building Ordinance

The commissioners and staff members expressed appreciation for Marie's service and acknowledged her contributions to the city. They also discussed various projects and initiatives, including the redevelopment of the Grand and Victoria area.

A motion was made to approve Resolution 25-1960, which approved the establishment of a tax increment financing district and related documents. The resolution passed with five in favor and one opposed (Council Member Bowie).

The meeting concluded with a recess, followed by a reconvening to discuss further resolutions at the city council meeting.
