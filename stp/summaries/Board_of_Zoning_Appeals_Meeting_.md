#### Meeting Info
* **Meeting Type:** Board of Zoning Appeals Meeting
* **Purpose:** Consideration of zoning variances and appeals
* **Date/Time:** March 12, 2023, 6:00 PM
* **Location:** City Hall, Saint Paul


#### Summary
The Board of Zoning Appeals Meeting was called to order at 6:02 PM. The first item on the agenda was a request for a zoning variance by a property owner in the Summit-University neighborhood. The applicant sought permission to build a three-story residential building with six units, citing a need for additional housing in the area.

After some discussion, the board voted 4-1 to approve the variance, with one member dissenting due to concerns about increased density and potential strain on local infrastructure. Another applicant presented a request to rezone their property in the Frogtown neighborhood from commercial to industrial use.

The board also discussed several other items, including:

* A proposed amendment to the city's zoning ordinance regarding accessory dwelling units
* An appeal of a denial of a zoning variance by a homeowner in the Highland Park neighborhood

No contentious votes were taken on these items. The meeting adjourned at 8:45 PM.

**Action Items:**

* Approve variance for Summit-University residential building (4-1 vote)
* Continue discussion on proposed amendment to zoning ordinance regarding accessory dwelling units
* Hear additional testimony on appeal of denied zoning variance in Highland Park neighborhood
