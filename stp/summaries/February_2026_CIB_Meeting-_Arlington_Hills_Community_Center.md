#### Meeting Info
**Meeting Type:** City Council Committee Board (CIB) meeting
**Purpose:** To discuss and vote on various proposals, projects, and initiatives affecting the city
**Date/Time:** February 10, 2026, at 3:00 PM
**Location:** Arlington Hills Community Center

#### Summary

The CIB meeting was attended by council members and several citizens. The main topics discussed included:

* **Budget Allocation for Public Safety:** A proposal to reallocate $1 million from the general fund to support additional police officers in high-crime areas was approved unanimously.
* **Zoning Ordinance Changes:** A proposal to update zoning regulations to allow for more mixed-use development projects was voted on, but not without controversy. Council member Johnson expressed concerns that the changes would lead to over-development and increased traffic congestion. The vote was 5-2 in favor of the proposal, with members Johnson and Thompson opposing it.
* **Neighborhood Street Improvements:** A request from the Frogtown neighborhood association for improvements to 4th Street, including repaving and lighting upgrades, was approved. The city has allocated $250,000 for these improvements.

The meeting concluded after approximately two hours of discussion and voting.
