#### Meeting Info
* **Meeting Type:** Special City Council Meeting
* **Purpose:** To honor the recipients of the 2025 Saint Paul Business Awards
* **Date/Time:** November 25, 2025, 1:00 PM
* **Attendees:** Members of the Saint Paul City Council, business award winners and nominees, and staff

#### Summary
The meeting was a celebration of the 2025 Saint Paul Business Award winners. The council president thanked the selection committee and the team that made the event possible.

There were five awards given out:

1. **Traditions Award:** Italian Pie Shop (Ward 3) for being a cherished part of the community for decades.
2. **People's Choice Award:** LifeJuices (Ward 1) for its commitment to wellness and providing healthy options.
3. **New Kid on the Block Award:** Lost Fox (Ward 2) for bringing a fresh, creative presence to downtown and Lower Town.
4. **Alice O'Brien Award:** Vikings and Goddesses Pie (Ward 4) for their commitment to equity and empowerment.
5. **Good Neighbor Award:** Roots Wellness Center (Ward 4) for their dedication to improving the community through mental health support and culturally grounded care.

Each award winner was introduced and had a chance to share a few words about their business.

After the awards, the council president moved for approval of the resolution, which passed unanimously. The meeting adjourned with a vote of appreciation for the winners and those who worked tirelessly to promote the awards program.

There were no contentious discussions or votes during the meeting.
