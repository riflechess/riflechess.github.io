#### Meeting Info

* **Meeting Type:** Housing & Redevelopment Authority Meeting
* **Date/Time:** May 13, 2026, 2:00 PM
* **Location:** City of Saint Paul (Virtual meeting)

#### Summary

The Housing and Redevelopment Authority (HRA) meeting discussed two major items: the Economic Development Strategy Update and the Afton View Apartments Project.

**Economic Development Strategy Update**

* Director Lloyd presented an update on the economic development strategy, a comprehensive plan to promote equitable and sustainable growth in Saint Paul.
* The consultant, TIP Strategies, is working with HRA staff to develop a data-driven plan that includes goals, objectives, and implementation strategies.
* Key areas of focus include:
	+ Small business and lending support
	+ Land use and redevelopment
	+ Commercial corridors revitalization
	+ Workforce development
* The consultant will be meeting with commissioners individually for feedback and input.

**Afton View Apartments Project**

* The developer, Tereen Development Partners, is requesting preliminary approval of multi-family conduit revenue bonds to finance the acquisition and rehabilitation of Afton View Apartments.
* The project includes 286 units, all of which will be affordable at 60% AMI for at least 40 years. 268 units are reserved for project-based vouchers.
* The developer committed to communicating with residents during construction, including tenant notices in Somali.

**Notable Discussion**

* Commissioner Yang asked about the level of specificity expected from the economic development strategy report and received clarification that it should include tangible, measurable goals and implementation strategies.
* Commissioner Noecker asked about the impact of project-based vouchers on affordability and was assured that if the property is not renewed for project-based vouchers, all units will remain affordable at 60% AMI.

**Resolutions and Motions**

* Resolution RES 26-786: Preliminary approval of multi-family conduit revenue bonds for Afton View Apartments was approved with a vote of 6-0.
* No action was taken on the Economic Development Strategy Update, as it is an ongoing project.
