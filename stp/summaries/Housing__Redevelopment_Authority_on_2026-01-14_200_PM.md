#### Meeting Info
* **Meeting Type:** Housing Redevelopment Authority meeting
* **Date/Time:** January 14, 2026, 2:00 PM
* **Attendees:** Vice Chair Jost, Commissioner Kim (absent), Commissioner Johnson (excused)
* **Purpose:** Review and approve spending plans for two redevelopment projects in Saint Paul

#### Summary
The Housing Redevelopment Authority met to review and approve spending plans for two projects. Here are the key decisions:

* Resolution 26-52: Approved a TIF loan extension for the 652 Sherburn Project, District 7, Ward 1. The resolution was adopted with no opposition.
* Resolution 2: Approved additional expenditures for the redevelopment of 560 Brunson Street under the Inspiring Communities Program, District 5, Ward 2. Commissioner Noecker made a motion to approve this item and thanked Director Musaw for his presentation.

There were no contentious votes or significant disagreements during the meeting. The commissioners discussed possible topics for future meetings, which are due by end of day Thursday, and will be compiled by Melanie Johnson for review. The meeting adjourned with all attendees thanking each other.
