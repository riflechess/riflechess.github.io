#### Summary

The meeting began with recognition of service by outgoing members and a warm welcome to new members. The discussion focused on the NSCC's community safety initiatives, including professional service agreements (PSAs) with organizations providing re-entry services, opioid response training, and youth justice programs.

**Key Decisions:**

* The council agreed to focus on prevention and intervention strategies to address community safety concerns.
* Tyler provided an update on the PSAs, highlighting the importance of quarterly reporting and transparency in community safety initiatives.
* Council members discussed the need for a stronger network of organizations working together to address common goals.

**Controversy:**

* The council debated the role of police presence in addressing community safety concerns. Some members emphasized the need for police intervention, while others highlighted the limitations of resources and the importance of prevention strategies.

**Future Plans:**

* Council members agreed to prioritize problem properties, police engagement, and community events as key areas of focus.
* Tyler suggested creating a phone tree or other communication system to reach out to community groups and encourage involvement in NSCC initiatives.
* John announced that he will be chairing the next meeting, and staff emphasized their commitment to working with council members to address community safety concerns.

**Notes:**

* Council members expressed appreciation for the open door policy and encouraged stakeholders to share concerns or issues directly with them.
* The meeting concluded with a call for healthy debate and transparency in decision-making processes.
