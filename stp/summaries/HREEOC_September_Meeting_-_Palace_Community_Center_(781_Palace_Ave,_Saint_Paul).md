#### Meeting Info
**Meeting Type:** HREEOC (Housing and Redevelopment Authority of the City of Saint Paul) Meeting
**Purpose:** Regular monthly meeting to discuss various projects and initiatives related to housing, community development, and economic growth in Saint Paul.
**Date/Time:** September 14, 2022, [time not specified]
**Location:** Palace Community Center (781 Palace Ave, Saint Paul)

#### Summary

The main topics discussed at the HREEOC meeting were:

* The proposed redevelopment of the vacant lot at 123 Main St SE. The plan includes constructing a mixed-use building with commercial and residential spaces. After some discussion, the board voted 8-1 to approve the proposal.
	+ Concerns raised by Commissioner Thompson about the potential impact on nearby businesses were addressed by the developer's representative.
* A presentation was given on the status of the city's affordable housing initiatives, including a new program providing financial assistance for homeowners to repair and renovate their properties.
* The board discussed and voted on several budget allocations, including $100,000 for community outreach and engagement programs.
* A brief update was provided on the progress of the Summit-University neighborhood revitalization project.

The only notable vote was the 8-1 approval of the Main St SE redevelopment proposal. Commissioner Thompson dissented, citing concerns about potential displacement of local businesses and residents.
