#### Meeting Info

* Type: Reparations Commission Meeting
* Purpose: To discuss various items on the agenda, including the Ramsey County Community Appropriate Response initiative and the draft procedural governance for consideration to incorporate into the bylaws.
* Date/Time: (Not specified)
* Location: Frogtown Community Center, 230 Como Ave, Large Meeting Room

#### Summary

The meeting began with a discussion of the Ramsey County Community Appropriate Response initiative. Dr. Raj presented on the initiative and its goals, highlighting the importance of community responders in addressing mental health and crisis situations. The commission discussed how to move forward with the initiative, including proposing it as a resolution to the city council.

Regarding the draft procedural governance for consideration to incorporate into the bylaws, Commissioner Robinson suggested adding a section that would allow new ideas to be brought forward at every meeting. This proposal was met with support from other commissioners, who noted that the current system has not been utilized effectively.

The commission also discussed amending the work plan to omit the data collection committee. Some commissioners expressed concerns about creating a separate committee for data collection, while others suggested that the archive committee could handle this task.

In addition to these discussions, the meeting included updates from various committees, including the harm report committee and the archive committee. The commission also received updates on the anti-black hate crime resolution, which is moving forward with support from council members.

The meeting concluded with a discussion of upcoming meetings and potential agenda items. Commissioner Robinson suggested presenting on community impact notes (racial equity impact assessments) at a future meeting, while another commissioner expressed interest in having the police internal review commission present to the board.
