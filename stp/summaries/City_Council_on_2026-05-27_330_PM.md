#### Meeting Info
**Meeting Type:** City Council Meeting
**Date/Time:** 2026-05-27, 3:30 PM
**Location:** City Hall, St. Paul, Minnesota
**Summary of Key Discussions and Decisions**

#### Summary
The meeting started with the approval of consent agenda items 2 through 11, except for items 6 and 8 which were pulled for separate consideration.

* **Item 6: Resolution 26-874**: The council approved Mayor's Appointment of Iye Zhang to serve as the City's Director of the Department of Safety and Inspections.
* **Item 8: Ramsey County Economic Development Authority**: A one-week layover was moved to allow the county to finalize their request.

The council also discussed and voted on several resolutions:

* **Resolution 26-841 (Auto Repair Garage)**: The department recommended a $1,000 matrix penalty and amended license conditions. After hearing from the appellant, the council approved the resolution.
* **Resolution 26-812 (Legislative Hearing Fee Waiver Pilot Program)**: A pilot program to waive fees for legislative hearings was approved, pending implementation of an ordinance to follow up on this resolution.
* **Resolution 26-791 (Appointment of Mellie McMahon as Director of Planning and Economic Development)**: After a discussion about the culture and values guiding economic development in St. Paul, the council voted to approve the appointment.

The public hearing section of the agenda included:

* **Grand Old Day Sound Level Variances**: The council approved sound level variances for Grand Old Day event on June 7th.
* **Climate Action and Resilience Plan Update**: The council adopted the Climate Action and Resilience Plan update, which includes a five-year action plan and prioritizes vulnerable communities.

The legislative hearing consent agenda was also approved, with amendments and recommendations from the legislative hearing officer.

The meeting concluded with "Good News from the Wards," where Council Members shared updates on local events and projects.
