#### Meeting Info
Meeting Type: Housing & Redevelopment Authority (HRA) meeting
Date/Time: 2026-04-01, 2:00 PM
Purpose: Review of HRA's activities and updates on various projects
Attendees: Commissioner Yang, Commissioner Noecker, Commissioner Coleman, Commissioner Necker, Chair Johnson, Director McMahon, Ms. Lorenz (HRA staff)

#### Summary

**Health Homes and Power of Home Programs Update**

* The Healthy Homes program provides pre-weatherization services for low-income homeowners in St. Paul.
* The Power of Home program provides electrification and electric-ready services for single-family homes.
* Both programs offer up to $50,000 in grant funding per home.
* As of now, 29 homes are in the Healthy Homes program, and 15 homes are in the Power of Home program.

**HRA-owned Land Disposition or Development Plan**

* The HRA owns about 200 parcels of property across six wards.
* Staff has been focusing on two types of properties: transfer properties (30 remaining) and developable properties (150).
* Transfer properties involve transferring small pieces of land to the city for infrastructure purposes, while developable properties are being developed or sold.

**Small-Scale Development Update**

* The HRA's small-scale development team has completed over 240 Inspiring Communities projects since 2013.
* These projects have provided affordable housing options for income-qualified buyers.
* The team is exploring new ways to sell land, including direct sales and community-driven RFPs.

**Additional Notes**

* Commissioner Noecker expressed concerns about the transfer of properties to the city, citing potential financial losses.
* Commissioner Bowie asked about previous transactions with MNDOT and county governments.
* Chair Johnson adjourned the meeting after discussing various updates and answering questions from commissioners.
