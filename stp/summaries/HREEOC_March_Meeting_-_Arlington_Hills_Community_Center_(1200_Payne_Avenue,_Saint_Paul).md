#### Summary

The HREEOC meeting focused on several key items related to housing development in Saint Paul. Some of the main topics discussed include:

* Approval of the Oak Hill Village redevelopment project: The committee approved a $3 million bond allocation for the project, which aims to transform an existing apartment complex into affordable and mixed-income housing.
* Update on the 7th Street Improvement Project: City officials provided an update on the ongoing construction project, which includes new streetscaping, public art installations, and affordable housing units. The project is expected to be completed by the end of 2023.
* Discussion of affordable housing metrics: Committee members discussed the city's progress in meeting its affordable housing goals, including the percentage of affordable units included in new developments.

There was no significant contention or disagreement during the meeting. However, some committee members expressed concerns about the pace of construction on the 7th Street Improvement Project and requested regular updates on the project's progress.
