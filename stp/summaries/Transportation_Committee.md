#### Meeting Info
**Type:** Transportation Committee meeting
**Purpose:** To discuss transportation projects, proposals, and issues in Saint Paul
**Date/Time:** Wednesday, March 15th, 6:00 PM - 8:30 PM
**Location:** City Hall, Room 300

#### Summary
The Transportation Committee met to discuss several key topics related to the city's infrastructure. Here are the main points:

* **Light Rail Extension**: The committee approved a proposal to extend the light rail line by 1 mile to connect with the Mall of America. This will require a budget allocation of $50 million.
* **Bike Lane Proposal**: A new bike lane was proposed on Grand Avenue, but some members expressed concerns about parking and traffic flow. The proposal was tabled for further discussion.
* **Snow Removal Funding**: The committee approved an additional $1 million to improve snow removal services in the city.

Some disagreement arose during the discussion of the bike lane proposal. Committee member Sarah Johnson argued that the bike lanes were necessary to promote alternative transportation, while member Tom Harris expressed concerns about parking and traffic congestion. Harris stated, "We can't just focus on one mode of transportation; we need to consider the impact on all users." The proposal was tabled for further discussion.

The meeting also saw some public testimony from residents concerned about road conditions and pedestrian safety.
