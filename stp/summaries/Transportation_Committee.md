#### Meeting Info
**Meeting Type:** Transportation Committee Meeting
**Purpose:** Review of proposed road improvements, bike lane expansions, and public safety initiatives
**Date/Time:** Tuesday, March 21st, 6:00 PM
**Location:** City Hall, Room 200

#### Summary
The Transportation Committee meeting on Tuesday, March 21st, covered several key topics. The committee discussed the proposed expansion of the Grand Avenue bike lane, with some members expressing concerns about increased congestion and parking difficulties.

* **Grand Avenue Bike Lane Expansion**: A motion was made to approve the expansion, but it failed by a vote of 4-3, with Councilor Johnson opposing due to potential traffic impacts. Committee Chair, Councilor Thompson, argued that the bike lane would improve safety and reduce emissions.
* **Road Improvement Projects**: The committee approved funding for several road improvement projects, including resurfacing of Snelling Avenue and construction of a new pedestrian crossing on University Avenue.
* **Public Safety Initiatives**: The committee also discussed proposals to increase law enforcement presence in high-crime areas and implement improved lighting along major corridors.

A significant portion of the meeting was devoted to public comments, with residents expressing concerns about traffic safety and bike lane usage. Some residents argued that the city should prioritize road improvements over bike infrastructure.
