#### Meeting Info
**Meeting Type:** ACOA (Action Committee on Aesthetic Ordinances)
**Purpose:** Regular meeting to discuss and address aesthetic concerns in Saint Paul neighborhoods
**Date/Time:** January 21, 2026, at an unspecified time
**Location:** Frogtown Community Center (230 Como Ave, Saint Paul, MN 55103)

#### Summary
The ACOA meeting covered several topics related to maintaining the aesthetic quality of Saint Paul's neighborhoods. Key decisions include:

* **Budget Allocation**: The committee allocated $10,000 for streetlight pole decorations in Rondo Avenue.
* **Tree Planting**: ACOA approved a proposal to plant 20 new trees along University Avenue by spring.
* **Neighborhood Clean-up**: The committee planned a neighborhood-wide clean-up event on February 14th.

One contentious topic was the **Street Sign Replacement**. Members of the committee debated whether to replace existing street signs with more modern designs, but ultimately decided against it due to concerns about historical accuracy and potential costs.

Public safety was discussed briefly, with ACOA members expressing support for increased lighting in high-crime areas.
