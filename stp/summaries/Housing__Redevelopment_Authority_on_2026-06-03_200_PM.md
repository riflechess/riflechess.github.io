#### Meeting Info
**Meeting Type:** Housing and Redevelopment Authority (HRA) meeting
**Date/Time:** June 3, 2026, 2:00 PM
**Purpose:** Public meeting for the HRA to discuss various items, including resolutions and staff reports.

#### Summary

The meeting began with the approval of minutes from the previous meeting. The main agenda items were:

1. **Resolution RES 26-889**: The HRA exercised its authority to opt into and support the Ramsey County Economic Development Authority (EDA). After a discussion and amendments, version two of the resolution was approved.
	* Key points:
		+ A million dollars will be allocated for economic development instead of affordable housing.
		+ The EDA and HRA will have a five-year partnership.
2. **Resolution 26-792**: Melanie McMahon was appointed as the Executive Director of the Housing and Redevelopment Authority of the City of St. Paul, Minnesota. Version two of the resolution was approved.
3. **Staff Report SR26-111: Galtier Plaza TIF District**: The HRA will provide tax increment financing (TIF) for a project to convert an office building into 166 units of market-rate housing.
	* Key points:
		+ The project includes a Skyway level and public area improvements.
		+ The developer, Beagle Scalteer LLC, will issue a TIF note up to $4.56 million.
4. **Staff Report SR26-112: Acquisition of 259 University Avenue West**: The HRA will acquire the property at 259 University Avenue West for redevelopment.
	* Key points:
		+ The property is currently occupied and has significant code deficiencies.
		+ The acquisition price is $600,000, which was funded through a Community Development Block Grant (CDBG).
5. **Staff Report SR26-113: Highland Bridge Update**: Sean Ryan provided an update on the Highland Bridge development, including progress on the Albert building, retail spaces, and residential units.

The meeting concluded with no major contentious issues or disagreements among the commissioners. The resolutions were approved, and the staff reports were accepted for further consideration at the next meeting.
