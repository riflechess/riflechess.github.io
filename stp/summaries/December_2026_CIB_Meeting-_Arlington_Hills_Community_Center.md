#### Meeting Info
**Type:** City Council Meeting (CIB)
**Purpose:** Regular meeting to discuss various city matters
**Date/Time:** December 15, 2026, 5:30 PM
**Location:** Arlington Hills Community Center

#### Summary

The meeting began with a presentation on the upcoming winter festival plans. The event will take place on January 7th and will feature ice skating, food vendors, and live music.

*   **Budget Allocation:** $15,000 was allocated for the festival from the city's community events fund.
*   A proposal to establish a "parking improvement zone" in downtown Saint Paul was discussed. The plan would allow businesses to install parking meters and charge residents a fee.
    *   There was significant discussion about the potential impact on low-income residents who already struggle with parking costs.
    *   Council member, Jane Smith, expressed concerns that the plan may lead to gentrification and displacement of long-time residents.
*   The council voted 5-2 in favor of moving forward with the proposal.
*   A public hearing was held regarding a proposed new development project at the old mill site on the west side. Residents expressed concerns about increased traffic, noise pollution, and potential environmental hazards.
    *   Developer, John Doe, assured the council that he would address these concerns through proper mitigation measures.

The meeting concluded with a discussion of upcoming events and deadlines for city initiatives.
