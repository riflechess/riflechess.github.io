#### Summary
The Reparations Commission meeting focused on several key topics:

* **Reparations Fund Allocation**: The commission voted to allocate $500,000 from the city's budget for reparations initiatives. This was met with some opposition from Commissioner Johnson, who argued that the amount was too low and did not adequately address the historical injustices faced by communities of color.
* **Community Engagement**: The commission discussed ways to increase community engagement in the reparations process, including holding town halls and creating a community advisory board.
* **Reparations Framework**: The commission reviewed and revised the city's reparations framework, which outlines the principles and goals for reparations initiatives.

Commissioner Rodriguez expressed concerns about the lack of representation from indigenous communities in the reparations process. Commissioner Lee countered that efforts are being made to engage with indigenous communities and address their specific needs.

The meeting ended with a sense of progress on some fronts but also acknowledged ongoing challenges and areas for improvement.
