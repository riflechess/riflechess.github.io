#### Meeting Info
**Meeting Type:** CIB (Community Investment Budget) Committee Meeting
**Purpose:** Review and discuss budget proposals for various community programs and initiatives in the City of Saint Paul
**Date/Time:** January 12, 2026, at 9:00 AM
**Location:** Arlington Hills Community Center

#### Summary
The CIB Committee met to review and discuss proposed budgets for various community programs. Key topics included:

* **Budget Allocations:** The committee reviewed budget proposals from several departments, including Parks and Recreation, Public Works, and Planning and Economic Development.
* **Arlington Hills Community Center Renovations:** A proposal was presented to renovate the Arlington Hills Community Center, which includes funding for new playground equipment, improved accessibility features, and upgraded lighting.
* **Community Engagement Initiatives:** The committee discussed proposals for community engagement initiatives, including increased funding for arts programs and expanded outreach services.

Notable points of contention arose during discussions on budget allocations. Ward 3 representative expressed concerns about the allocation of funds for the Arlington Hills Community Center renovations, suggesting that the costs may be too high and potentially diverting resources from other essential projects. The matter was tabled pending further review.

The committee voted to approve the proposed budgets with some amendments, including increased funding for community engagement initiatives. However, Ward 1 representative dissented on one specific line item related to arts program funding, citing concerns about the potential impact on local businesses and taxpayers.
