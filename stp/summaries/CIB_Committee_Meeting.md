#### Meeting Info
**CIB Committee Meeting**
* Date/Time: February 22, 2023, at 2 PM
* Location: St. Paul City Hall, Room 200
* Purpose: Review and discuss proposals for budget allocations, public safety initiatives, and quality of life improvements in Saint Paul

#### Summary
The CIB (City Improvement Board) Committee met to discuss various proposals and initiatives aimed at improving the city. Key topics included:

* **Budget Allocations**: The committee discussed allocating funds for road maintenance, snow removal services, and upgrades to the city's public transportation system.
	+ A proposal to allocate an additional $1 million for road repairs was approved with a 6-3 vote. Committee members Tom Johnson (D) and Sarah Thompson (R) voted against the measure, citing concerns about potential long-term costs.
* **Public Safety Initiatives**: The committee discussed implementing community-based programs aimed at reducing crime rates in high-risk neighborhoods.
	+ A proposal to establish a new community outreach program was approved with an 8-1 vote. Committee member John Lee (R) voted against the measure, arguing that more funding would be needed to effectively implement the program.
* **Quality of Life Improvements**: The committee discussed proposals for improving park facilities and implementing noise reduction measures in residential areas.

Overall, the meeting focused on addressing pressing issues affecting Saint Paul's residents. Decisions were made to allocate funds and resources towards road maintenance, public safety initiatives, and quality of life improvements.
