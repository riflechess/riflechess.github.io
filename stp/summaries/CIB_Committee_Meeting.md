#### Meeting Info
**CIB Committee Meeting**
**Date:** March 10, 2023
**Time:** 2:00 PM
**Location:** City Hall, Room 200
**Purpose:** Regular meeting of the Community Investment Budget (CIB) Committee to discuss budget allocations and projects for the city.

#### Summary
The CIB Committee met on March 10, 2023, to review and approve budget proposals for various city departments. The key discussions and decisions were:

* **Budget Allocations:**
	+ The committee approved a $1 million increase in funding for the Parks and Recreation department to support new community center projects.
	+ A $500,000 allocation was approved for the Public Works department to upgrade street lighting infrastructure.
* **Prioritized Projects:**
	+ The committee prioritized 5 proposals for new affordable housing initiatives, with a total budget of $2.5 million.
	+ A proposal to establish a community garden program in the North End received approval with a budget of $150,000.

There was some discussion around the allocation of funds for the public works department, with some committee members expressing concerns about the need for more transparency in budgeting processes. However, no formal opposition or votes were recorded on this issue.

The meeting concluded with a brief discussion on potential new projects and initiatives that will be brought forward at future meetings.
