#### Meeting Info
**Type:** MACPD (Minneapolis Advisory Committee on Problem Properties) Meeting
**Purpose:** Discuss public safety concerns, problem properties, and community development in the West Minnehaha area
**Date/Time:** January 2026, [specific date/time not provided]
**Location:** West Minnehaha Rec Center

#### Summary
The MACPD meeting focused on addressing public safety concerns and community development in the West Minnehaha area. Key topics included:

* A proposal to install additional streetlights along Minnehaha Avenue to improve visibility and reduce crime.
	+ Council Member Rodriguez expressed support, citing improved safety for residents and businesses.
	+ Council Member Lee raised concerns about potential environmental impact and sought more information on energy-efficient options.
* Discussion on the renovation of the West Minnehaha Rec Center, including plans for a new community garden and outdoor recreational space.
	+ Council Member Patel highlighted the importance of inclusive design and accessibility features in the renovation.
	+ Council Member Nguyen emphasized the need to involve local residents in the decision-making process.
* Review of problem properties in the area, including a vacant lot on 32nd Street that has been a concern for neighbors due to litter and vandalism.

A vote was held to allocate funds for the streetlight installation, with a majority (5-2) supporting the proposal. Council Members Lee and Rodriguez dissented, citing concerns about environmental impact and potential budget overruns. The renovation plans for the West Minnehaha Rec Center will be further discussed at the next MACPD meeting.
