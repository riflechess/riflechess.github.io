#### Meeting Info
**Meeting Type:** City Council meeting
**Date/Time:** June 3, 2026, at 3:30 PM
**Location:** City of Saint Paul


#### Summary

The city council meeting started with a brief discussion on the consent agenda, where item 2 was pulled for separate consideration. The Midway Men's Club presented their donation to the city and expressed gratitude for the opportunity to speak.

Key decisions made during the meeting include:

* Approving the rezoning of property at 213 Bates Avenue from H2 Residential to T1 Traditional Neighborhood
* Exercising the city's authority to opt into and support the Ramsey County Economic Development Authority
* Granting a sound level variance for the Hot Summer Jazz Festival event on June 19-20 at Mears Park
* Approving the application of the United Hmong Family Inc. for a sound level variance for their Hmong International Freedom Festival event on June 26-28 at Como Regional Park
* Authorizing the Department of Parks and Recreation to accept grant funding from the Minnesota Department of Natural Resources for tree planting at the Como Zoo

The legislative hearing consent agenda was also adopted as amended.

In terms of public safety, there was a discussion on sound level variances for various events. The United Hmong Family Inc. requested a 120 dba sound variance for their event to ensure public safety and clear announcements.

The meeting concluded with announcements from the wards, including a soapbox derby event and the official opening date for the Hamlin Midway Library.

Note: There was no significant disagreement or contentious issues raised during the meeting. The decisions made were largely unanimous, with some discussion on minor details.
