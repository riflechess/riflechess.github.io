#### Meeting Info

* Type: Housing and Redevelopment Authority meeting
* Purpose: To discuss and vote on various items related to housing redevelopment, TIF districts, and public amenities
* Date/Time: December 10, 2025, 2:00 PM
* Relevant Contextual Information: The meeting was attended by Chair Johnson, Commissioners Bowie and Kim were excused

#### Summary

The Housing and Redevelopment Authority (HRA) met to discuss various items related to housing redevelopment, TIF districts, and public amenities. Key decisions include:

**Resolution 25-1908:** Approved the extension of current contracts with parking ramp management companies for one year.

**Public Hearings:**

* Resolution 25-280: Authorized the sale and conveyance of a vacant lot at 77 Congress Street East to Iglesia Niqlito Church.
* Resolution 25-278: Authorized the conveyance of three parcels located in Chestnut Plaza to the City of St. Paul.

**Staff Reports:**

* Introduction to Ames Lake Property, RWMWD Flood Mitigation Project: The HRA is supporting a partnership with the watershed district to improve flood mitigation and create public amenities on an HRA-owned property.
* Grand and Victoria Redevelopment, TIF District: The HRA will consider approval of a TIF development agreement for the project, which includes a six-story mixed-use building with 90 rental apartments and commercial space.

**Controversy:**

* The developer, Ari Parrots, addressed concerns about the necessity of public support for the Grand and Victoria redevelopment project. He stated that even market-rate projects require public assistance due to the lack of new supply in the area.
* Commissioner Noecker expressed concern about the precedent set by the project, but the developer assured that every project must stand on its own merits.

**Budget Allocations:**

* The TIF plan for the Grand and Victoria redevelopment project includes estimated tax increments totaling $363,076 annually, beginning in 2030.
