#### Meeting Info

**Location Context:** City of Saint Paul

#### Summary
**4D Property Tax Program Discussions**
The board engaged in a detailed discussion regarding "4D" properties—affordable housing units that currently benefit from an 80% property tax reduction. The conversation focused on the logistics of when a property transitions from a rental (eligible for the tax break) to owner-occupied (ineligible). 

Specifically, members inquired about how the city handles mid-year sales. There was a discussion regarding whether there is a "clawback" process if an owner receives the full year's tax benefit but sells the property and removes it from the affordable housing program shortly after. Staff noted that while the city provides the compliance list to the state, the specific mechanics of tax adjustments are managed at the county/state level. Staff will follow up with state partners to clarify these details for future consistency.

**Land Conveyance and Redevelopment (1087 Ross Avenue)**
The board held a public hearing regarding Resolution 26-145. This resolution involves:
*   The sale and conveyance of an HRA-owned parcel located at **1087 Ross Avenue** (District 4, Ward 6).
*   The authorization to enter into a development agreement and the allocation of expenditures for the redevelopment of this site.

**Key Decisions & Votes**
*   **Authorization Release:** A motion to move forward with specific administrative actions following the tax discussion was approved unanimously (6-0).
*   **1087 Ross Avenue Development:** The public hearing resulted in a unanimous vote (6-0) to approve the sale of the property and the associated redevelopment expenditures.
