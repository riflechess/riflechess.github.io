#### Meeting Info
* **Meeting Type**: Housing and Redevelopment Authority meeting
* **Purpose**: To discuss and vote on various resolutions related to housing projects in Saint Paul
* **Date/Time**: February 11, 2026, 2:00 PM
* **Location**: Live and Recorded Public meetings of Housing & Redevelopment Authority for City of Saint Paul

#### Summary
The meeting started with a roll call, where it was noted that Chair Johnson is excused and Commissioners Bowie and Kim are absent.

* The first discussion item, Resolution 26-252, was approved unanimously (5-0) to extend authorization of funds for certain St. Paul projects applying for funding from the Minnesota Housing Finance Agency (MHFA). This resolution acknowledges that reservations made in June 2025 for round one of the Consolidated RFP remain in place for round two.
* The meeting ended with a note that next week's HRA meeting on February 18th has been canceled, and the next meeting will take place the following week.

There were no contentious or divided votes during this meeting.
